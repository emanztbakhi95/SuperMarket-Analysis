SuperMarket-Analysis
# Project Objective:
This project analyzes sales data to identify top-performing products across different cities, analyzed by month and customer gender.

# Key Analysis:
- City-Based Targeting: Identifying the most popular products per region.
- Temporal Trends: Analyzing sales fluctuations across different months.
- Demographic Insights: Understanding buying patterns based on Gender.

# Tools used: 
- Language: Python
- Libraries: Pandas / Matplotlib
- Methodology: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

# Quick Insights:
- All branches show that January is the peak sales season, while February is the Lowest selling month.
- Most sales among females are in the "Food and Beverages" section, while the lowest are in "Health and Beauty." For males, the highest sales are in the "Health and Beauty" section and the lowest are in the "Sports and Travel" section.
- The highest sales in the "Alex" branch were in the "Home and lifestyle" section and the lowest in the "Health and Beauty" section, while the "Cairo" branch had the highest sales in the "Sports and Travel" section and the lowest in the "Food and Beverages" section, and finally the "Giza" branch had the highest sales in the "Food and Beverages" section  and the lowest in "Home and lifestyle" section
- The highest sales among member customers were in the "Food and Beverages" section, while the lowest were in the "Electronics and Accessories" section. Similarly, the highest sales among regular customers were in the "Electronics and Accessories" section, while the lowest were in the "Health and Beauty" section.
